Tech for all.

We believe in the transformative power of technology. Through computer donations and computer science lessons, we are expanding access to technology one community at a time.

Our Projects

DLP is based in Cambridge, Massachusetts. We are committed to providing computers and computer science lessons to middle schools in the Greater Boston Area. In 2010 and 2011, we ran two pilot programs (linked) in grades four through six, where we used XO Laptops (linked) to teach students about the power and versility of technology. In fall 2013, we are expanding our pilot programs to three to five local middle schools.
