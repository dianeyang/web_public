Katelyn Foley

Co-Chairman, katelyn@DigiLiteracy.org

Katelyn Foley is a media and entertainment strategist with experience in management consulting and industry. She is currently in her second year of USC's Peter Stark Producing Program, a hybrid business and media production graduate program. Most recently, Katelyn worked at Google in YouTube monetization strategy and independently produced a branded web series for Subway. Prior to USC, Katelyn was a consultant at Bain & Company in Los Angeles, where she focused on digital and social media strategy for Fortune 500 brands and helped start Bain's first formalized practice area in these fields. She graduated from Harvard College with high honors in 2009.

MacKenzie Sigalos

Co-Chairman, mackenzie@DigiLiteracy.org

MacKenzie Sigalos graduated from Harvard (Magna Cum Laude) in 2010 and she later earned her Masters Degree from the University of Oxford. After finishing her graduate studies, she worked for CNN on Anderson Cooper’s nightly news program, based out of New York City. She is currently working for CNN International in their Hong Kong bureau. A co-founder of DigiLit, MacKenzie has combined her passion for education with her work with multimedia technologies.
